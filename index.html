WE ARE Pak Afridi Enterprises

Ghani Brothers Enterprises was incorporated in 1996 in Karachi, to provide oil transportation, with the idea of giving most advance and reliable service for transporting oil and allied products throughout the Pakistan.

As our profile moves you will find how different innovative steps were brought into practice first time in Pakistan regarding safe, efficient and economic way of transporting oil and allied products. Recently we incorporated new firm by the name of PAK AFRIDI ENTERPRISES.

Now we operate by Ghani brothers Enterprises and Pak Afridi enterprises. Today we are one of the leading oil transportation firms in Pakistan, transporting both crude and refined oil throughout the country for almost all the leading oil marketing, exploring and refining companies.
